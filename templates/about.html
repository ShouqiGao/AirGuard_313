About AirGuard

Our Mission

AirGuard is a real-time air quality monitoring platform dedicated to providing transparent, accessible air quality data for Malaysia. We believe that clean air is a fundamental right, and that informed citizens can make better decisions about their health and the environment.

Our platform empowers individuals, communities, and policymakers with real-time air quality information, enabling proactive measures to protect public health.

Core Features

  • Real-Time AQI Monitoring: Get hourly air quality updates for most cities
  • Location-Based Detection: Automatically detect and display air quality for your current location
  • Multi-City Comparison: Compare air quality across different cities to make informed decisions
  • Quick Search: Seamlessly search and monitor multiple locations
  • Query History: Track your search history and monitor trends over time
  • High Performance: Optimized concurrent queries and intelligent caching for fast results

Data Sources & Attribution

AirGuard wouldn't be possible without the dedication of environmental organizations committed to transparency and public health.

🌍 Malaysia Department of Environment (DOE)

Official Data Provider: The DOE is Malaysia's leading environmental agency, responsible for monitoring and protecting the nation's air quality.

They provide comprehensive air quality data collected from monitoring stations across Malaysia, ensuring accurate and authoritative information for the public.

Visit DOE Official Website →

📊 AQICN (Air Quality Index China Network)

Global Air Quality Data: AQICN operates one of the world's largest air quality information networks, aggregating data from thousands of monitoring stations globally.

Their mission is to increase public awareness of air pollution and enable people around the world to evaluate and understand air quality information.

Visit AQICN Website →

Privacy & Transparency

AirGuard respects user privacy while maintaining the transparency necessary for environmental protection.

  • We only collect IP addresses for location detection - never sold or shared with third parties
  • Query history is stored securely in our database and used only for analytics and improvement
  • All data processing is done securely within our infrastructure
  • We support both IP-based and manual location searches for maximum privacy control
